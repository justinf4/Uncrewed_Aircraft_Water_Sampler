Table 1.   Component List

Component Number Component Role and Key Specifications
1 Microcontroller: Arduino Nano ESP32
  • ESP32-S3 dual-core Xtensa LX7 @ 240 MHz
  • 512 KB SRAM, 16 MB flash, 8 MB PSRAM
  • Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n, Bluetooth LE 5.0
  • I2C, SPI, UART, 14 digital + 8 analog GPIO
  • 3.3 V logic
  • USB-C
2 Sensor: DS18B20 Temperature Probe
  • Measures temperature
  • 1-Wire digital interface (single data pin, 4.7 kΩ pull-up)
  • Range: −55 to +125 °C
  • Accuracy: ±0.5 °C
  • Programmable 9–12-bit resolution (0.0625 °C at 12-bit)
  • 3.0–5.5 V
3 Sensor: GPS Module
  • Measures latitude, longitude, altitude, speed, and UTC time
  • UART serial, default baud rate 9600 bps (adjustable)
  • Update rate 1 Hz (configurable)
  • 3.6–5 V supply; active IPX ceramic patch antenna
  • Onboard EEPROM saves config; TinyGPS++ Arduino library
4 Receiver: FrSky X8R 2.4 GHz
  • 16-channel RC telemetry receiver
  • ACCST protocol
  • 8 PWM outputs (CH1–CH8) + SBUS port (CH1–16)
  • Smart Port full-duplex telemetry
  • RSSI PWM output (0–3.3 V)
  • Operating voltage 4.0–10 V, 100 mA @ 5 V
  • Range: >1.5 km
5 Transmitter: TX16S
  • 16-channel RC transmitter used for drone flight control
  • Sampler uses one toggle switch that outputs a PWM signal on channel 1
6 Electronic: SD Card Logger
  • MicroSD card read/write for CSV data logging
  • SPI interface (MOSI, MISO, SCK, CS)
  • Onboard 3.3 V regulator and level shifter
  • Supports FAT16/FAT32; Arduino SD.h + SPI.h libraries
7 Actuator: HSR-2648CR
  • Continuous-rotation digital servo (360°)
  • Speed proportional to PWM deviation from 1500 μs center (50 Hz)
  • Metal gears, dual ball bearings, 25T spline
  • Wide voltage 4.8–7.4 V; ~12 kg·cm torque @ 7.4 V
8 Switch: IP68 Reed Switch
  • Magnetically actuated normally-open (NO) switch
  • Closes in the presence of a magnetic field; digital GPIO input
  • Used for contactless position sensing
9 Switch: IP68 Toggle Switch
  • Manual on/off for device
10 Power: 12.6 V 3-Cell Li-ion Battery
  • 5 A max; powers system independent from drone
11 Power: 12V–5V Buck Converter
  • Converts 12 V to 5 V at 3 A from the battery to the power rail
12 Power: 8 A Fuse
  • Compact glass fuse for circuit protection
13 Fabrication: 3D Printed Parts
  • White PETG used with Bambu Labs P1S and A1 Mini (FDM printer)
  • Sampler body and electronics bracket
  • 0.1–0.2 mm tolerance
